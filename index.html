Related Works

  • Ethics of Advanced AI Assistants

    • Advanced AI assistants are advanced natural language interfaces that can autonomously plan and execute tasks on behalf of users, potentially transforming work, education, and personal interactions. However, the paper highlights some serious ethical and societal challenges which includes issues of value alignment, safety, trust, and misuse that must be carefully addressed through coordinated technical, policy, and evaluative efforts.

  • LLASM: Large Language and Speech Model
  • NLP for older people's speech patterns
    • The ways that older people interact with voice assistants can vary drastically compared to their younger counterparts. As people age, their cognitive abilities decrease and the way that they are expected to speak to voice assistants may start to stray from the truth. This paper looks into how to better address this change in speech patterns in order to create an accurate and effective voice assistant for all types of consumers.
  • Introduction to NLP
    • This paper gives an in-depth introduction to what natural language processing is. It gives advice on different issues that you will face while working with NLP alongside different terms and ways to structure a program that can correctly understand language.
  • Real-Time Speech Emotion Analysis for Smart Home Assistants
    • This paper explores an AI-driven system for detecting emotions in speech. Using Mel Frequency Cepstral Coefficients (MFCCs) for feature extraction and a convolutional neural network (CNN) for classification, the system enhances smart home assistants by improving emotional awareness and response accuracy. The research highlights the integration of Hidden Markov Models (HMMs) and covariance matrices to refine emotion recognition in real-time. This could be helpful in making our speech bot obtain more "witt" like the ones in the movies.
